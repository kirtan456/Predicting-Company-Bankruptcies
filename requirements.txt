Here's a brief description of some key categories:
Profitability Ratios: 
Various Return on Assets (ROA) measures
Operating Gross Margin
Operating Profit Rate
Net Interest Rates (pre-tax and after-tax)
Growth Rates: 
Sales Growth Rate
Profit Growth Rates (Operating, After-tax, Regular, Continuous)
Asset and Net Value Growth Rates
Per Share Metrics: 
Earnings Per Share (EPS)
Cash Flow Per Share
Revenue Per Share
Net Value Per Share
Liquidity Ratios: 
Current Ratio
Quick Ratio
Cash Ratio
Solvency and Leverage Ratios: 
Debt Ratio
Interest Coverage Ratio
Debt to Equity Ratio
Efficiency Ratios: 
Asset Turnover Ratios
Inventory Turnover
Accounts Receivable Turnover
Fixed Asset Turnover
Cash Flow Indicators: 
Cash Flow to Sales
Cash Flow to Assets
Cash Flow to Liability
Operational Efficiency: 
Revenue per Person
Operating Profit per Person
Working Capital Metrics: 
Working Capital to Total Assets
Inventory to Working Capital
Other Financial Indicators: 
Tax Rate
Research and Development Expense Rate
Degree of Financial Leverage (DFL)
